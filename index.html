Do More.

DotBox is a simple, physical tool. It helps you set and achieve your goals.

How it works

Write a goal on a dot. Up to four.
Set the goal by roating the dot to orange.
When you complete a goal, turn its dot to blue.

DotBox for kids

Kids (and parents) love the DotBox. Kids prefer stickers when setting their goals.
A fun set of stickers is included with each DotBox.

Use the tool your way

Most people set about four goals per week. Kids usually use the DotBox as a daily checklist. DotBox is flexible. Use it in a way that fits your schedule and your approach.
